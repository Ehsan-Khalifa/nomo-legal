# Delete your NoMo account

This page is the account-deletion resource for the **NoMo** app
(package `com.banana.nav`).

---

## Delete from inside the app (fastest)

1. Open NoMo and go to the **Profile** tab.
2. Scroll down and tap **Delete account**.
3. Confirm.

This **permanently** deletes your account and all data associated with it on
our servers — profile, saved places, alarms, trip and search history, app
settings, emergency contacts, and your profile photo — and clears the app's
data stored on your phone. Deletion is immediate and **cannot be undone**.

## Delete without the app installed

Email **nomo.dev001@gmail.com** from the email address on your NoMo account
with the subject line **"Delete my account"**. We verify the request by
replying to the account's address, then delete the account and all associated
data. We aim to complete verified requests within **7 days**.

## What is deleted, and what may be kept

| | |
|---|---|
| **Deleted** | Account, profile, saved places, alarms, history, settings, emergency contacts, profile photo — everything tied to your user ID. |
| **Kept** | Nothing tied to your identity. Aggregated, de-identified crash/diagnostic events (if any) cannot be linked back to you. Records we are legally required to retain (e.g. subscription/billing records held by Google Play) are kept only as long as the law requires. |

## Deleting some data without deleting your account

Saved places, alarms, fences, and emergency contacts can each be removed
individually inside the app; removals sync to our servers. Uninstalling the
app removes all app data stored on your device.

## Subscriptions

Deleting your NoMo account does **not** cancel a Google Play subscription —
cancel it in the Play Store (Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions)
before or after deleting, or you may keep being billed by Google.
