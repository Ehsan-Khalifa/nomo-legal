NoMo includes **automated features that act on sensor and location data**. These

- **Location alarms** depend on your device's GPS, network, battery, operating

- **Crash detection** uses your device's motion sensors and a threshold-based
  heuristic. It **may fail to detect a real crash, or may falsely trigger** on

  SIM, and network signal — **delivery is not guaranteed.**
- **Live Share and SOS** relay location over the network and depend on
  connectivity of every participant. Positions may be stale or unavailable.

NoMo does not monitor you, does not call emergency services, and cannot
guarantee that any alert, message, or location will be delivered, delivered on
